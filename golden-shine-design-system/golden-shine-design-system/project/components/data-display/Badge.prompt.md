Small pill label — for "Most popular", "Insured", "Eco-friendly", availability, etc. Soft tint by default; `solid` for emphasis.

```jsx
<Badge tone="coral" solid>Most popular</Badge>
<Badge tone="success">Available today</Badge>
<Badge tone="navy">Insured & bonded</Badge>
```

Tones: `navy`, `coral`, `cream`, `success`, `warning`, `danger`, `neutral`.
